About Us

Purpose-Driven, People-Focused Logistics

Themba Liyeza Logistics is a dynamic South African transport and logistics company founded in 2018 by Nomathemba Skhosana Selesho, a visionary entrepreneur from Bronkhorstspruit. Built on principles of resilience, inclusion, transformation, and excellence, we've grown from a small trucking operation to a multi-service logistics powerhouse serving clients across Gauteng, Mpumalanga, and beyond.
